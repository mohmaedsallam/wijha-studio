Privacy Policy

1

Data Collection

unfinished does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your data — including your unfinished items, decisions, emotional weight scores, preferences, and settings — is stored locally on your device only.

2

Local Storage

Your items, decisions, emotional weight scores, photos, and preferences are saved using on-device storage (MMKV). This data never leaves your device. We cannot access, read, or recover your data — it is entirely under your control.

3

Photos

Photos you add to items are stored locally on your device's filesystem. They are not uploaded, transmitted, or accessible to anyone but you. Photos are only used within the app to display alongside your items.

4

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time through the app settings or your device settings.

5

Analytics & Tracking

unfinished does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or any form of usage monitoring. We do not track your behavior, usage patterns, or any other activity within or outside the app.

6

Third-Party Services

In-app purchases are processed securely through the Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). Payment information is handled entirely by these platforms — we never see, collect, or store your payment details. No other third-party services are used that collect user data.

7

Children's Privacy

unfinished does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to function. It is safe for users of all ages.

8

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it at all times. You can delete individual items within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling the app. No data exists on any external server to delete.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
